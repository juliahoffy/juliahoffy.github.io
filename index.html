All About My Dogs

By: Julia Hoffberger

Hi! I'm Julia Hoffberger and I love dogs. I have 3 dogs named Bucky, Shanie, and Lily. They are all Labradoodles and the best dogs ever! I couldn't imagine my life without them.

Reasons why I love dogs:

  • They are so loveable
  • They give everyone so much company
  • So fun to play with
  • Make everyone happier overall!
